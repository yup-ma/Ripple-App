Community Guidelines

Last updated August 1, 2022

In order to keep the Ripple community safe, fair, and fun for everyone we created community guidelines. If you see content that you believe violates any of these guidelines, please report it or downvote it and our moderators will review it promptly.



Personal information

  • If someone is not a public figure, do not release their private information. Private information includes but is not limited to names, contact information, phone numbers, addresses, and social media handles.
  • Do not use identifiable information about someone in the community in a negative context.

Hate speech

  • Do not post anything that engages in prejudice based on race, class, age, ethnicity, gender, or sexual orientation.

Harassment and bullying

  • Do not post anything that could be interpreted as bullying or harassing an individual or a group. This includes but is not limited to rumors, body shaming, and using derogatory names toward a person or group.

Misinformation

  • Do not intentionally post misinformation or information that is deceiving.
  • Do not impersonate other people when posting.

Spam and promotion

  • Do not post content that has no substance.
  • Do not post the same or similar content multiple times within a short duration.
  • Do not promote products that are not beneficial to the community.

Democratic Interference

  • Do not use the platform to obstruct justice or participation in an election or other civic responsibility.

Violence and threats

  • Do not post anything violent including words and images.
  • Do not threaten anyone.

Suicide and self-harm

  • Do not post or make jokes about harming yourself.
  • Do not encourage others to harm themselves.

Drugs, alcohol, and illegal activities

  • Do not ask for drugs or alcohol on the platform, indirectly or directly.
  • Do not ask for help in committing illegal activities.

Sexual content

  • Do not post anything that could be interpretted as promoting non-consenual sexual acts.
  • Do not pressure anyone into sexual acts through the platform and do not solicit sex.
  • Do not post post pornographic content.

These guidelines are not exhaustive, and Ripple Interactive, Inc. reserves the right to remove content that we believe harms the community.



Failure to abide by these community guidelines may result in a temporary or permanent account suspension.
